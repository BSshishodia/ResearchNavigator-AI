How PaperIQ Works: Interactive Guide

Welcome to PaperIQ: Automated Research

PaperIQ is your comprehensive research partner. We combine powerful deep learning models with Gemini's intelligence and permanent MongoDB storage to transform how you analyze and synthesize academic literature.

This platform is built on Flask, advanced HuggingFace Transformers (BART), and secure MongoDB persistence.

The Core NLP Pipeline

1. Extractive Summary

Uses TextRank (Graph theory) to select the most important original sentences.

2. Abstractive Summary

Uses BART-Large-CNN to generate human-readable, synthesized text.

3. Readability & Metrics

Calculates Flesch-Kincaid Grade and ROUGE quality scores.

4. Keyword & Entity

KeyBERT and spaCy identify core concepts and named entities for visualization.

AI Synthesis & Multi-Analysis

The AI Research Assistant is powered by **Gemini** with Google Search Grounding. This allows you to:

  • **Search:** Find academic papers using natural language.
  • **Select:** Choose up to 5 documents from the web.
  • **Synthesize:** Gemini combines all sources into one master report summary.
  • **Analyze:** The full NLP pipeline runs on the *synthesized* report for detailed metrics.
